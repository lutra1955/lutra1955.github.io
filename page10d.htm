The Wied, 1998
The Wied. A stream where one can fish in a relaxed way.

After reading an article about the Wied in the magazine of the Dutch Fly Fishers Association, my friend Frans and I decided to check it out. The date was Thursday August 20th 1998.
It turned out to be a lovely stream. Fellow anglers who only want to catch trout and grayling better try their luck elsewhere, but those who have pleasure in catching a nice chubb can have a good time here.
From central Holland, you get there by following the A3 (E35) via Arnhem, Oberhausen and Koln up to exit number 35 Neustadt/Wied.


At Neustadt the river looks quite promising, but we followed the river upstream. In the village of Burglahr one can get a day ticket at hotel Burghof, for 18 DM. The beat is 2 km long and wading is possible over nearly the entire length. There are lots of chubb, schneider, roach and barbel.
The Wied at Dottesfeld. Very few trout but many chubb. We saw a kingfisher several times.
Next day we went further and ended up at hotel Zum Wiedbachtal in Dottesfeld. Here you can get a day permit for 17 DM and the beat is about 4 km long. Once again, many chubb, schneider, roach and here Frans got the only trout of the trip. The kitchen of Hotel Zum Wiedbachtal has a very good reputation. Guests of the hotel also can fish the Holzbach, a brook which runs nearly in front of the hotel.

Hotel Zum Wiedbachtal, Fam Bollander. Tel.: (00-49) 2685-1060.
