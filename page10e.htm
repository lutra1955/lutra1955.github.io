Bavarian forest 1998

Summer holiday 1998 was spent in Bischofsmais in the Bavarian Forest - South East Germany. A beautiful area. There are a lot of things to see and to do. A visit to the National Park Bayerischer Wald is worth the effort, all kinds of animals which belong in this area can be seen here. For anglers a visit to the hunting- and fishing museum in Freyung is very interesting.
We stayed in an appartment of the holiday park in Bischofsmais. A little river runs near the village, the Schlossauer Ohe. The clear water of the narrow and overgrown river makes the angling difficult, but very interesting.
A permit can be purchased at the Tourist Information in the village. The charge is 15DM per day (1998). If you are here for a holiday with the family, it can be useful to bring along a little flyrod.
